Flower Advice

Choosing flowers is about emotion, not rules. Here are a few gentle tips to guide you.

  • Red Roses
    A timeless symbol of deep love and passion. Red roses are often chosen for anniversaries, Valentine’s Day, or meaningful romantic gestures. “I ordered red roses for our anniversary — they said everything I felt without a single word.”
  • Tulips
    Simple, warm, and sincere. Tulips are perfect for expressing care, appreciation, and gentle affection. “Tulips felt honest and personal. Not too much, just perfect.”
  • Lilies
    Elegant and expressive, lilies symbolise admiration, respect, and refined beauty. Often chosen for important life moments. “The lilies looked graceful and calm — exactly the mood I wanted.”
  • Sunflowers
    Bright and uplifting, sunflowers represent happiness, optimism, and positive energy. “The sunflowers instantly brought a smile. They felt like pure joy.”
  • Peonies
    Soft, romantic, and luxurious. Peonies are associated with love, prosperity, and celebration. “Peonies made the bouquet feel special and unforgettable.”
